Cilantro Lime Chicken Bowl

Cook Time: 35 Minutes




Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 teaspoon cumin

  • 1 teaspoon paprika

  • 1 teaspoon chili powder

  • Salt and black pepper to taste

  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

  • Juice of 2 limes

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or rice

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

  • 1 avocado, sliced

  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed

  • Greek yogurt or sour cream for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.

  2. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, minced garlic, cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.

  3. Rub the spice mixture over the chicken breasts.

  4. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through.

  5. While the chicken is cooking, prepare quinoa or rice according to package instructions.

  6. Once the chicken is done, let it rest for a few minutes before slicing into strips.

  7. In a large bowl, toss the cooked quinoa or rice with chopped cilantro and lime juice.

  8. Assemble the bowls by dividing the quinoa mixture among serving bowls.

  9. Top with sliced grilled chicken, cherry tomatoes, avocado slices, and black beans.

  10. Serve with a dollop of Greek yogurt or sour cream on the side.

  11. Enjoy your flavorful Cilantro Lime Chicken Bowl!
