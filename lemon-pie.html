Lemon Meringue Pie Bliss

Prep Time: 30 Minutes | Bake Time: 15 Minutes | Chill Time: 2 Hours




Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-baked pie crust

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar

  • 1/3 cup cornstarch

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1 1/2 cups water

  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter

  • 4 large egg yolks, beaten

  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • For Meringue:
  • 4 large egg whites

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt.

  2. Gradually whisk in water and lemon juice until smooth.

  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture comes to a boil.

  4. Stir in butter until melted.

  5. Gradually whisk about 1/4 of the hot mixture into the beaten egg yolks, then add the egg yolk mixture back to the saucepan.

  6. Cook, stirring constantly, until thickened.

  7. Remove from heat and stir in lemon zest and vanilla extract.

  8. Pour the lemon filling into the pre-baked pie crust.

  9. For the meringue, beat egg whites with an electric mixer until foamy.

  10. Add cream of tartar and continue beating. Gradually add sugar, one tablespoon at a time, until stiff peaks form.

  11. Spread the meringue over the hot lemon filling, sealing the edges of the pie crust.

  12. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 12-15 minutes or until the meringue is golden brown.

  13. Chill the Lemon Meringue Pie in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.

  14. Enjoy your delightful Lemon Meringue Pie!
