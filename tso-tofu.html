General Tso's Tofu

Cook Time: 45 Minutes




Ingredients:

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed

  • 1/2 cup cornstarch

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce

  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce

  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

  • 2 tablespoons sugar

  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic

  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger

  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)

  • 3 green onions, chopped

  • Sesame seeds for garnish

  • Cooked white or brown rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Toss the pressed and cubed tofu in cornstarch until evenly coated.

  3. Bake the tofu on a lined baking sheet for 25-30 minutes or until golden and crispy.

  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, minced garlic, minced ginger, and red pepper flakes.

  5. In a pan, heat the sauce mixture until it thickens.

  6. Add the baked tofu to the pan, coating it in the sauce.

  7. Stir in chopped green onions and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  8. Serve the General Tso's Tofu over cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds.


Enjoy your flavorful General Tso's Tofu!
