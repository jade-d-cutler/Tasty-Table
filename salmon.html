Ginger Garlic Baked Salmon

Cook Time: 35 Minutes




Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 2 tablespoons honey

  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar

  • Salt and black pepper to taste

  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. Place salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, olive oil, honey, grated ginger, minced garlic, sesame oil, and rice vinegar.

  4. Brush the ginger garlic mixture over the salmon fillets, ensuring they are well-coated.

  5. Season the salmon with salt and black pepper to taste.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.

  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions before serving.

  8. Enjoy your flavorful Ginger Garlic Baked Salmon!
