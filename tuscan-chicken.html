Tuscan Baked Chicken

Cook Time: 1 Hour




Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

  • 1/4 cup olive oil

  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary

  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced

  • 1/2 cup artichoke hearts, chopped

  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish.

  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, and oregano. Season with salt and pepper.

  4. Brush the olive oil mixture over the chicken breasts.

  5. Scatter cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, and artichoke hearts around the chicken.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.

  7. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the top and bake for an additional 5 minutes until the cheese is melted.

  8. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.


Enjoy your flavorful Tuscan Baked Chicken!
