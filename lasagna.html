Chicken Pesto Lasagna

Prep Time: 20 Minutes | Bake Time: 40 Minutes




Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked al dente

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken

  • 1 cup pesto sauce

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese

  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

  • 1 egg, beaten

  • 3 cups spinach, chopped

  • 2 cups marinara sauce

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. In a large bowl, combine shredded chicken, pesto sauce, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, beaten egg, chopped spinach, salt, and pepper.

  3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce in the bottom of the prepared baking dish.

  4. Place three lasagna noodles over the sauce, and then layer half of the chicken-pesto mixture on top.

  5. Repeat with another layer of noodles and the remaining chicken-pesto mixture.

  6. Top with a final layer of lasagna noodles and cover with marinara sauce.

  7. Sprinkle additional mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.

  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Allow the lasagna to cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.


Enjoy your Chicken Pesto Lasagna creation!
