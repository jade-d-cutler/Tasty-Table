Salvadorian Pupusas

Cook Time: 30 Minutes




Ingredients:

  • 2 cups masa harina

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water

  • 1 cup refried black beans

  • 1 cup shredded queso fresco

  • 1 cup finely chopped loroco (optional)

  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine masa harina and warm water. Knead until you have a smooth dough. Add more water if needed.

  2. Divide the dough into golf ball-sized portions.

  3. Flatten each ball into a disk and add a spoonful of refried black beans, shredded queso fresco, and loroco (if using) in the center.

  4. Fold the dough over the filling, sealing the edges to form a stuffed ball.

  5. Flatten the stuffed ball into a thick pancake shape, ensuring the filling is evenly distributed.

  6. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium heat. Cook pupusas for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cooked through.

  7. Serve pupusas warm with salsa and curtido (pickled cabbage slaw).


Enjoy your delicious Pupusas!
