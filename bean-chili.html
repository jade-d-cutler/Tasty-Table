5 Bean Chili Sensation

Cook Time: 1 Hour




Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed

  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed

  • 1 can (15 oz) pinto beans, drained and rinsed

  • 1 can (15 oz) navy beans, drained and rinsed

  • 1 can (15 oz) garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained and rinsed

  • 1 lb ground beef or plant-based alternative

  • 1 large onion, diced

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes

  • 2 cups vegetable broth

  • 2 tablespoons chili powder

  • 1 tablespoon cumin

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef (or plant-based alternative) over medium heat.

  2. Add diced onions and minced garlic. Cook until onions are translucent.

  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, and all the beans.

  4. Season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.

  5. Cover and let the chili simmer for at least 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  6. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve hot.


Enjoy your hearty 5 Bean Chili!
