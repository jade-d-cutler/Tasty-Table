Mushroom Swiss Burger Bliss

Cook Time: 30 Minutes




Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 4 hamburger buns

  • 4 slices Swiss cheese

  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced

  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

  • 1 tablespoon butter

  • Fresh lettuce, tomato slices, and condiments for topping

Instructions:

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Season with salt and pepper.

  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the burger patties to your desired doneness.

  3. During the last few minutes of cooking, place a slice of Swiss cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.

  4. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and sauté sliced mushrooms and onions until softened.

  5. Stir in Worcestershire sauce and butter into the mushroom and onion mixture.

  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the Swiss cheese-topped patties on buns, and topping with the sautéed mushrooms and onions.

  7. Add fresh lettuce, tomato slices, and your favorite condiments.

  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your Mushroom Swiss Burger!
